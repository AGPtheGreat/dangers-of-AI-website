Accessibility Statement

Commitment to Accessibility

System Daemons is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Standards and Guidelines

Our website aims to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).

Accessibility Features

  • Alt text for images
  • High color contrast
  • Semantic HTML structure

Known Limitations

While we strive to adhere to accepted accessibility standards, we recognize that some areas of the site may not yet be fully accessible. We are currently addressing the following:

  • Adding contact information and accessibility statement links to each page.
  • Implementing keyboard navigability.
